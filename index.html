Open Source Tools - Research Group

Open science requires that knowledge will be made accessible for everyone. Open source software (FOSS) can generate software that fully satisfy the requirements of different user types. Also, allows the scientists to kept under control their research process, which is compatible with the ethos of academic autonomy that is proper to researchers.

Our research group on Open Science Tools want to provide software and specifications useful to researchers of different disciplines. Our team includes specialist from Psychology, Economy, Biodiversity, Software Engineering and Communication, to provide a broad and comprehensive vision of the needs of the scientific community.

Our main project is Buhos, an application for managing the complete process of systematic literature reviews that is web-based and developed in Ruby. We are working towards extending its features, to make it a complete solution for different type of literature reviews and meta-analysis. Also, we need help to improve the user experience of the application.

You could contact us on our Mailing group or sending an e-mail.
